About Me

Computer Engineering @ University of Waterloo

Hello! My name is Daniel Kwan and I am currently pursuing my Bachelor's of Honours Applied Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo. My interests span across machine learning, full stack development, and cybersecurity.

I grew up in Vancouver, Canada, and from a young age I was always curious about how things worked. I loved coding and spent a lot of time exploring software and cybersecurity, mostly out of genuine curiosity and the satisfaction of figuring things out.

In high school, I found balance through sports, especially volleyball. Playing competitively taught me a lot about teamwork, communication, and staying calm under pressure. I'm also a black belt with 12 years of karate training, which has taught me discipline and perseverance. Another major part of my growth came from being a police cadet with the Vancouver Police Department, where I served as a corporal.

I love making new projects that actually interest me and matter. Currently, I'm working on making machine learning models from scratch. One of my biggest hobbies right now is playing poker with friends because of the amount of math, critical thinking, and analysis you need to do in the game.
