02 — TRANSMISSIONS

  • Aegis - YHack Winner

    OSINT · TF-IDF · YHACK 2026 WINNER

    A real-time OSINT tool that flags identity links — recurring time, location, and activity patterns that let an adversary reconstruct your routine from public posts alone. NER, OCR, and EXIF extraction feed three TF-IDF vector spaces, scored by cosine similarity into a weighted breach probability. YHack 2026 winner at Yale.

  • ExoDiscover - NASA Space Apps Finalist

    XGBOOST · LIGHT CURVES · NASA SPACE APPS

    An exoplanet classification system trained on NASA's Kepler and TESS catalogs. Statistical, transit, and periodicity features are extracted from raw light curves and fed to an XGBoost classifier — with CNN and LSTM variants for sequence-level analysis — to label each target as confirmed, candidate, or false positive.

  • Midas

    YOLOV11 · GPT-4O VISION · QHACKS 2026

    An AR repair assistant for broken devices, optimized for mobile. YOLOv11 labels components with real-time bounding boxes, GPT-4o Vision diagnoses the damage, and voice-guided repair steps flow through Whisper speech-to-text and OpenAI TTS — React 19 in front, FastAPI behind.

  • Sign CLI

    CNN · MEDIAPIPE · UOTTAHACKS 2026

    A real-time ASL recognition engine: MediaPipe hand tracking feeds a custom convolutional network that classifies the full A–Z alphabet plus space and delete. Wrapped in a learning game with structured lessons, XP and streaks, and per-letter weakness detection backed by a local SQLite database.

  • Forecast FM

    SCIKIT-LEARN · FASTAPI · SPOTIFY

    A weather-conditioned music classifier: scikit-learn models predict a weather category for any song from its audio features — valence, energy, tempo, acousticness, loudness — pulled through the Spotify API. Served by FastAPI behind a React + TypeScript frontend.
